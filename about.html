PANORAMAS OF CINEMA 🍿
🪅 ABOUT

Panoramas of Cinema is the custom-made search engine of a collection of movies in love with architecture –PARADIS🍅. It deals with millions of images, video clips and dialogues, by mimicking the techniques deployed by Google Search in its handling of information.

PoC is a research project that addresses the challenges presented to architectural education and practice by the Internet era and its AI-powered applications. Its questions and unfoldings assist in educational projects of various architecture schools across Europe, including ETH Zurich, University of Innsbruck and TU Vienna.

Panoramas of Cinema is developed by Dr.Sc. Jorge Orozco at the Chair for Digital Architectonics in ETH Zurich's Department of Architecture.
